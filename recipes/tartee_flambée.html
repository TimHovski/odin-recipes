Tarte flambée

The basis of the tarte flambée is a very thinly rolled out base of bread dough. The traditional topping consists of raw onions, bacon and a cream of sour cream lightly seasoned with salt and pepper. The flatbread topped in this way is baked in the oven over high heat for a short time

Ingredients

  • 200g / 7oz flour
  • 2el / 2tsp oil
  • 125ml / 4 fl.oz
  • 200g / 7oz sour cream
  • 200g / 7oz onion
  • 100g / 3,5 oz bacon
  • salt
  • black pepper

    • Steps

    1. Make a kneading dough from flour, oil, water and salt; it shouldn't feel sticky.
    2. Thinly slice onions; Also cut the bacon into thin, narrow strips.
    3. Roll out the dough very thinly, spread with sour cream.
    4. Spread the onions and bacon on top.
    5. Bake in the preheated oven at the highest level for about 15 to 20 minutes until the base is crispy.
